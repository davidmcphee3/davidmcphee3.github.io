F1 2022 Cars

2022 is the first season that I am fully involved in with F1. An exciting part of the season is when the teams show off their car for the first time. Included is photos (with source) and some comments.

All images from formula1.com.

Mercedes

Constructors champions last year Mercedes will defend that title with the new regulations. The Mercees, along with most other teams are 'porpoising' this year. In videos the violent bouncing is clear and is terriable for the car. This is because at high speeds the downforce is great enough that the bottom of the car experiences ground effect. Basically the ground compromises the aerodynamics.

Red Bull Racing

Driver champions last year with some super undercut side pods. These cut side pods look to be a very recent addition because they are not even painted yet.

Ferrari

Meme king Ferrari, maybe this year is the year... They were top in the first practice and had ground effect problems to a much lesser extent than Merc.

McLaren

McLaren has been battling for best of the rest over the past couple of years but are looking to maybe shake up the top 3 this year around. They seem to be in a similar boat as Ferrari.

Aston Martin

Aston Martin really has had a glo-up since the Racing Point Days. Seb was the first driver to say that he would not race in Russia this year.

Apline

My wife's favourite, because it is pink. Apline use to be a nice blue but BWT took over as a major major sponser so I gues they get to add their own colours to it.

AlphaTauri

AlphaTauri is like Red Bull 2 but I really like Pierre Gasly so I am always cheering for them.

Alfa Romeo

I <3 the cameo livery. The livery is cameo because the Alfa car wasn't actually launched yet.

Williams

Battle for not last. Oh how Williams use to be so good.

HAAS

HAAS is looking like the back-marker again even though they basically skipped 2021 to develop this car. Fun fact this photo was taken right before Russia declared war on the Ukraine. HAAS is an american team but has a lot of connections to Russian sponsers. The biggest HAAS sponser is Uralkali which is owned by Mazepin's father. This is bad news for Mazepin who has been a 'pay driver' for a year now.
